About us

We’re two student brothers running The Vive — a youth-led resale business focused on quality, affordability, and sustainability. While we stay behind the scenes, our goal is simple: help great items find new life.

A Brief Overview

The Vive gives quality pre-owned items a fresh start, making sustainable shopping easy and affordable. We provide links to our Vinted and eBay pages as well.

  • Type: Resale Brand
  • Founded: 2025
  • Location: Online Only
  • Products: Clothing, Books, Everyday Finds, Accessories
  • Audience: Students & Smart Shoppers
  • Mission: Affordable, Sustainable, Reliable
  • Platform: Vinted, eBay and More
  • Run By: Student Team

We believe every item deserves a second chance, and every shopper deserves great value. That’s why we’re passionate about reducing waste and making smart, sustainable choices easy.

Our Process

Here's how we make second-hand shopping simple, sustainable and stylish.

Sourcing

We carefully select pre-owned items with potential — from fashion to everyday finds.

Cleaning & Checks

Every item is cleaned and checked to make sure it's in good condition.

Photography

We take clear, honest photos so buyers know exactly what they're getting.

Listing

Items are listed on our platforms(E.g eBay & Vinted) with detailed, honest descriptions.

Packaging

Orders are packaged securely using eco-friendly or reused materials when possible.

Shipping

Orders are sent promptly using the shipping options provided by Vinted or eBay.
